About Me

I recently graduated with an M.S. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems from Arizona State University, bringing a versatile background in embedded systems, computer vision, and autonomous navigation. My work spans real-time motion capture, multi-robot coordination, and autonomous vehicles — bridging hardware and software to build intelligent systems that operate in the real world.

Off the clock, I channel my competitive drive into basketball and snowboarding — proudly representing my state at national-level basketball competitions. These pursuits, alongside orchestrating complex team projects, have sharpened my discipline, strengthened my teamwork, and honed my leadership abilities, fueling my passion for transforming innovative ideas into real-world impact.
